A List of Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Son of Don Rafael Ibarra
  • At the beginning of the story, he returns to the Philippines after seven years of studying in Europe
  • Member of the Ibarra Family, a prestigious clan who is the richest clan in San Diego
Maria Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • Daughter of Kapitan Tiago de los Santos and Donya Pia Alba
  • Because of Donya Pia Alba's early death, Tiya Isabel took care of Maria Clara on her behalf
  • The love of Crisostomo Ibarra
Elias
  • He is Ibarra's Friend
  • His father died and his sibling went away
  • His mother is rich
Kapitan Tiago
  • He is a rich man in San Diego
  • He arranges the connection between Maria Clara and Crisostomo
  • His late wife, Donya Pia Alba is the mother of Maria Clara
Padre Damaso
  • He is the real father of Maria Clara
  • He treats his people with violence
  • He is a Franciscan priest
Padre Salvi
  • He is a sickly priest
  • He is Padre Damaso's replacement after he left San Diego
  • He punishes people with excessive taxes
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra
  • He was wrongfully accused of killing somebody
Pilosopo Tasyo o Don Anastacio
  • He is an old scholar
  • Despite his wife being gone for many years, he continues to search for her grave
Sisa
  • The mother of Basilio and Crispin
  • Used to be rich before marrying her husband
Crispin
  • The younger son of Sisa
  • Wrongfully accused of stealing money from the church
  • Killed by Sakristan Mayor under the command of padre Salvi
Basilio
  • The older son of Sisa
  • Escaped the convent and was shot in the process
Donya Victorina de Espadana
  • A friend of Kapitan tiago
  • Her husband Don Tiburcio
Don Tiburcio de Espadana
  • Husband of Donya Victorina
  • Friend of Kapitan Tiago
Donya Consolacion
  • The wife of the Alperes
  • She used to be a Labandera until she met her husband
Alperes
  • He is the leader of the civil guards
  • His wife is Donya Consolacion
Tiya Isabel
  • The aunt of Maria Clara
  • Cared for Maria Clara after her mother died
